DIET PLAN

A healthy immune system and the restoration of damaged cells can be aided by a well-balanced diet. It gives you the extra energy you need to deal with stressful situations.

Diet plan 1

Breakfast:-1 bowl of poha or daliya with vegetable.
Snack:-Mixed nuts (approx. 25g).
Lunch :-1-2 chapatis.1 small bowl of dal
1 small bowl of sabzi (carrot-caulifowerpeas) and 1 bowl of curd.
Snack:- 1 bowl of cut fruits/ 1 glass of fruit juice.
Dinner:- 1-2 chapatis,1 small bowl of dal
and 1 small bowl of sabzi (palak paneer)
